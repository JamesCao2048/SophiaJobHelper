# Sensitive Schools List & Academic Cooperation Risks

This document lists Chinese universities that are considered "sensitive" due to inclusion in the US Entity List, direct affiliation with the defense industry ("Defense Seven Sons"), or other sanctions. 

**Risk Factors for Applicants (especially with US background):**
1.  **Visa Restrictions (PP10043)**: High risk of F/J visa rejection for future visits to the US (conferences, exchange).
2.  **Export Control**: Restricted access to US-origin high-end hardware (GPUs) and software (MATLAB, EDA).
3.  **International Cooperation**: Difficulty in establishing joint projects or funding with US institutions.
